About

Starting in 2025, I serve as Director of Computer Vision on Sam's In-Club Tech at Walmart Global Tech. Previously, from 2022 to 2025, I was a Manager of Machine Learning on the Sam's AI Labs at Walmart Global Tech where my teams created and maintained the time series forecasting platform for finance, merchandising, supply chain, and in-store operation within Walmart.

Formerly, I was a team lead of algorithm in the machine learning research group at Texas Instruments, where I focused on hardware-software co-design for machine learning acceleration chips and developed chip-specific model training platforms for our customers.
